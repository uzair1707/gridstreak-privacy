Account & Data Deletion

GridStreak - Habit Tracking App

We respect your right to control your personal data. This page explains how to request deletion of your GridStreak account and all associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

Follow these steps to delete your account:

  1. Send an email to our support team at the address below
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request - GridStreak"
  3. Include the email address associated with your GridStreak account
  4. We will confirm receipt of your request within 48 hours
  5. Your account and data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers:

  • Account Information: Your email address and username
  • Habit Data: All habit names, types, and tracking configurations
  • Activity Logs: All daily check-ins, streak data, and progress history
  • Patterns: Your selected visual patterns and completion progress
  • Achievements: All unlocked badges and gamification data
  • Settings: Reminder preferences and app configurations

Data Retention Period

Once we receive and confirm your deletion request:

  • Your account will be deactivated immediately
  • All personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

Alternative: Delete Data Locally

If you only want to remove data from your device without deleting your account:

  • Open GridStreak app
  • Go to More ‚Üí Settings
  • Tap Sign Out to remove local data
  • Or uninstall the app to remove all local data

Note: This only removes data from your device. Your account and server data will remain intact.
